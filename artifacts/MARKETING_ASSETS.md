## ✍️ STORE PAGE COPY

### 1. Steam Store Page

#### Short Description
Descend into the shifting depths of a dying planet. In **VOIDCORE: DEEP ALCHEMY**, you will drill through hazardous biomes, extract unstable cosmic elements, and establish overlinks to surface terminals. Will you contain the reactor, absorb the void singularity, or trigger a perfect resonance stabilization?

#### Long Description
**VOIDCORE: DEEP ALCHEMY** is a high-stakes, atmospheric mining and extraction adventure. 

As a deep-surface engineer, your mission is simple: dig down, gather rare resources, buy state-of-the-art suit upgrades, and survive. But the deeper you drill, the more the planet pushes back. Survive extreme heat, security grid drainage, and dimensional fractures to reach the World Core and choose the fate of the planet.

#### Key Features:
*   **Futuristic Visor HUD**: Track your oxygen, cash flow, cargo capacity, and critical targeting systems directly through your high-tech miner visor.
*   **Deep-Core Progression**: Mine through 6 unique biomes, including the lava-filled *World Core*, the high-risk *Ancient Facility*, and the unstable *Reality Fracture*.
*   **Visceral Mining Feel**: Experience hit-flashes, screenshakes, combos, and satisfying physics hit-stops that make every strike feel punchy and rewarding.
*   **Dynamic Modifiers & Seeds**: Customize your descent. Enable *Low Gravity* for high impact, *Deep Darkness* to test your scanner, or *Hardcore Collapse* for the ultimate challenge.
*   **Multiple Ending Paths**: Secure the facility, absorb the rift energy, or achieve absolute harmony. Your stabilizer combination dictates your ending.
*   **Prestige System**: Return to the surface with permanent rank benefits, providing a compounding cash multiplier and tool efficiency upgrades.
